To get started modeling 3D objects: 
1) enter (3) 3D points' X, Y, and Z coordinates.
2) Hit "Add Triangle"
3) Observe that the triangle is created in the model below.

Wow! Your first 3D face! Cool!


Want to make a cube? 
1) Use 2 triangles to make a square
2) Repeat step 1 five times to create five more faces
3) That's it! A whole cube!

Note: making cubes will soon be much less tedious.
