1.Adding 3D objects: 
    1) Open the menu
    2) Open "Shape Creator" 
    3) Choose a Shape
    4) Add parameters


2. Moving the Camera
    A) Rotation
        1) Slowly click and drag the rendering window.
        2) In the menu, open the Camera settings and adjust manually.
    B) Translation
        Use WASD keys to move around the XZ plane.
        Note: this moves the camera relative to the world, not itself.


3. Changing the Light
    A) Color:
        1) Open the menu
        2) Open "Lights" box
        3) Enter a hex RGB color code (#00ff00: green)
