1. Exploring
    A) Controls
        1) Slowly click and drag the rendering window. 
           This will point the camera where you drag.
        2) Use W and S keys to move forward and backward.
        3) Use A and D keys to navigate the X-axis
        3) Use Up and Down arrow keys to navigate the Y-axis
    B) Menu
        1) In the menu, open the Camera settings and adjust manually.



2. Adding 3D objects: 
    1) Open the menu
    2) Open "Shape Creator" 
    3) Choose a Shape
    4) Add parameters



3. Changing the Light
    A) Color:
        1) Open the menu
        2) Open "Lights" box
        3) Enter a hex RGB color code (#00ff00: green)
